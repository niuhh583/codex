## Feedback Sorting

Classify feedback into:

- Must revise: factual errors, mandatory brand/legal requirements, confirmed client decisions.
- Strategic discussion: feedback that changes audience, objective, claim, route, or success criteria.
- Preference: subjective tone, visual taste, wording preference.
- Scope change: new deliverable, new channel, new round, new audience.
- Risk: timeline, budget, approval, compliance, production feasibility.

For each item, state the recommended response and impact on timeline, workload, or quality.
