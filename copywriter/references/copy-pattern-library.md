# Copy Pattern Library

This library provides reusable copy structures. Do not copy the example lines mechanically. Use the structures to translate strategy, insight, and product truth into concrete language.

## How to Use

1. Identify the task type with `copy-rule-trigger-map.md`.
2. Choose 2-4 relevant patterns from this file.
3. Generate multiple rough lines.
4. Use `copy-methodologies.md` to judge whether the lines are truthful, strategic, channel-fit, and persuasive.
5. Rewrite the best options until they feel specific, human, and brand-owned.

## Campaign / Slogan Patterns

### Pattern: 反常识重定义

Use when:

- A category has a stale default belief.
- The brand needs to own a sharper definition.

Core mechanism:

- Break the old frame, then offer a deeper or more useful frame.

Structure:

- 不是 A，而是 B。
- 你以为是 A，其实真正的问题是 B。
- 与其 A，不如 B。

Example style:

- 不是恢复原样，而是重新长回自己。
- 不是多一种选择，而是换一种判断标准。

Why it works:

- It creates cognitive contrast and gives the brand the power to redefine the category.

Avoid:

- Do not be contrarian for decoration. B must be more accurate and strategically useful than A.

### Pattern: 身份宣言

Use when:

- The audience wants recognition, belonging, or self-definition.
- The brand is building a tribe or worldview.

Core mechanism:

- Turn a product or campaign into a statement of who the user is.

Structure:

- 我不是 [旧标签]，我是 [新身份]。
- 写给 [正在经历某事的人]。
- [人群] 不需要 A，他们需要 B。

Example style:

- 写给那些正在把生活重新拿回手里的人。
- 她不是变脆弱了，她只是正在重新定义力量。

Why it works:

- People share copy that helps them say who they are.

Avoid:

- Do not assign an identity the audience would reject.

### Pattern: 敌人命名

Use when:

- The audience has a shared frustration, but no clear language for it.
- The campaign needs a common enemy.

Core mechanism:

- Give a name to the thing the brand and audience reject together.

Structure:

- 我们反对的不是 A，而是 B。
- 真正困住你的，不是 A，是 B。
- 别再让 [敌人] 定义你。

Example style:

- 真正困住她的，不是产后身体，而是所有人替她写好的身份。
- 别再让效率崇拜偷走你的呼吸。

Why it works:

- Naming the enemy creates alignment and emotional clarity.

Avoid:

- Do not invent an enemy that feels fake, exaggerated, or hostile without reason.

### Pattern: 动作化主张

Use when:

- The strategy is abstract and needs to become executable.
- The campaign needs an action, ritual, or participation hook.

Core mechanism:

- Convert a value into something the audience can do.

Structure:

- 把 [抽象价值] 变成 [具体动作]。
- 从今天开始，先 [一个小动作]。
- 用 [动作]，重新 [获得某种状态]。

Example style:

- 从今晚开始，把 15 分钟还给自己。
- 用一次安静的涂抹，把身体慢慢接回来。

Why it works:

- Actions make abstract brand values easier to remember and participate in.

Avoid:

- Do not create actions that are too complicated or unrelated to the product.

### Pattern: 旧词新解

Use when:

- The category depends on a familiar but tired word.
- The brand wants to make an old word feel newly meaningful.

Core mechanism:

- Keep the word, change its meaning.

Structure:

- 所谓 [旧词]，不是 A，而是 B。
- [旧词]，也可以是一种 [新解释]。
- 今天，我们重新理解 [旧词]。

Example style:

- 恢复，不是回到从前，而是重新拥有自己。
- 温和，不是无力，而是更懂身体的节奏。

Why it works:

- It lets the brand inherit category familiarity while changing the emotional territory.

Avoid:

- Do not redefine words without a real strategic need.

## Brand Manifesto Patterns

### Pattern: 时代情绪开场

Use when:

- The brand needs to speak from a cultural or generational tension.
- The audience shares a broad emotional climate.

Core mechanism:

- Begin with a feeling many people have but few brands name.

Structure:

- 这个时代，很多人都在 [共同状态]。
- 我们越来越 [现象]，却越来越 [矛盾]。
- 每个人都在谈 A，很少有人谈 B。

Example style:

- 每个人都在问效率，很少有人问我们还剩多少感受。
- 很多人都在谈恢复，却很少有人承认，真正丢失的是掌控感。

Why it works:

- It gives the brand a cultural point of view rather than a product-only opening.

Avoid:

- Do not use vague social commentary that could fit any brand.

### Pattern: 连续否定后转向

Use when:

- The category has several wrong assumptions.
- The brand needs to clear space before stating its belief.

Core mechanism:

- Reject what the brand is not, then land on what it believes.

Structure:

- 不是 A。不是 B。也不是 C。真正重要的是 D。
- 我们不相信 A，也不制造 B。我们只相信 C。

Example style:

- 不是奖励。不是补偿。也不是把你变回原来的样子。真正重要的，是让你重新拥有自己。

Why it works:

- It creates rhythm, removes category baggage, and makes the final belief feel earned.

Avoid:

- Do not overuse negation without landing on a strong positive claim.

### Pattern: 私人独白

Use when:

- The brand needs intimacy, vulnerability, or emotional depth.
- The audience needs to feel spoken to rather than marketed to.

Core mechanism:

- Write as if one person is speaking privately to another person.

Structure:

- 我知道你不是不想 A，你只是 B。
- 也许你从来没有说出口，但你一直在 [感受]。
- 这一次，我们想先问你一句：[问题]。

Example style:

- 我知道你不是不够坚强，你只是太久没有被允许先照顾自己。
- 也许你没有说出口，但你真的很想先把自己找回来。

Why it works:

- It lowers defense and creates emotional recognition.

Avoid:

- Do not fake intimacy or over-assume trauma.

### Pattern: 价值观递进

Use when:

- The brand needs a manifesto, proposition, or belief system.
- The message should move from truth to stance to action.

Core mechanism:

- Build a ladder: fact -> tension -> belief -> action.

Structure:

- 我们看见 [事实]。
- 我们不接受 [错误逻辑]。
- 我们相信 [价值观]。
- 所以我们选择 [行动/产品/服务方式]。

Example style:

- 我们看见身体在变化。我们不接受用焦虑解释这种变化。我们相信恢复应该从理解开始。所以隐山选择更温和、更长期的方式。

Why it works:

- It makes brand attitude feel grounded rather than decorative.

Avoid:

- Do not jump straight to values without showing the truth that created them.

## Social Hook Patterns

### Pattern: 痛点自白

Use when:

- The audience has an unspoken feeling.
- A social post needs immediate emotional recognition.

Core mechanism:

- Turn private discomfort into a first-person admission.

Structure:

- 我终于承认，[真实痛点]。
- 最难的不是 A，而是 B。
- 原来我一直在 [状态]。

Example style:

- 我终于承认，产后最难恢复的不是身材，是我对自己的掌控感。
- 最累的不是照顾别人，是忘了怎么照顾自己。

Why it works:

- Self-recognition creates saves, comments, and sharing.

Avoid:

- Do not dramatize pain beyond what the audience would accept.

### Pattern: 人群点名

Use when:

- The post is for a clear group.
- You need the user to feel selected.

Core mechanism:

- Name the audience and their current state.

Structure:

- 写给正在 [状态] 的 [人群]。
- 如果你是 [人群]，这件事你可能已经感受到了。
- [人群] 最容易忽略的，不是 A，而是 B。

Example style:

- 写给那些刚成为妈妈，却还想保留自己的女人。
- 给每个正在重建节奏的人。

Why it works:

- Specificity increases relevance and click intent.

Avoid:

- Do not use broad labels that feel like targeting jargon.

### Pattern: 反直觉提醒

Use when:

- The audience believes one thing, but the brand has a better explanation.
- The content needs a strong opening.

Core mechanism:

- Challenge a familiar assumption.

Structure:

- 你以为是 A，其实是 B。
- 别急着 A，先看看 B。
- 真正影响你的，可能不是 A。

Example style:

- 你以为产后恢复是把身体变回去，其实是把生活重新拿回来。

Why it works:

- It creates curiosity and reframes the user's problem.

Avoid:

- Do not challenge an assumption unless the content can prove the new view.

### Pattern: 场景瞬间

Use when:

- The copy needs realism and image.
- The audience problem is best felt through a scene.

Core mechanism:

- Enter through a small, recognizable moment.

Structure:

- 当你 [具体动作] 的时候，才发现 [真实感受]。
- 那一刻你突然意识到，A 不是 A，而是 B。
- 从 [时间/地点/动作] 开始，[变化] 出现了。

Example style:

- 凌晨三点醒来时，你才发现，最想找回的不是睡眠，而是属于自己的时间。

Why it works:

- Specific scenes make abstract emotion believable.

Avoid:

- Do not choose scenes that feel staged or over-written.

### Pattern: 问题钩子

Use when:

- The user already has a question in mind.
- You want to invite entry instead of push a claim.

Core mechanism:

- Put the user's unspoken question into words.

Structure:

- 为什么 [现象]？
- 有没有可能，[新解释]？
- 如果 A 不是答案，那 B 会不会更重要？

Example style:

- 如果产后恢复不是回到原样，那真正该恢复的是什么？

Why it works:

- Questions let users participate mentally before reading.

Avoid:

- Do not ask fake questions with obvious answers.

## Expression Craft Patterns

### Pattern: 对仗映衬

Use when:

- The line needs to be remembered quickly.
- A slogan, KV headline, festival poster, or brand proposition needs compact rhythm.

Core mechanism:

- Use structural, semantic, or rhythmic symmetry so two ideas reflect and strengthen each other.

Structure:

- A 的是 X，B 的是 Y。
- 不是 A 的 X，而是 B 的 Y。
- 平时 A，关键时 B。
- 越 A，越 B。
- 从 A 到 B。
- 少一点 A，多一点 B。
- 把 A 放下，把 B 捡起。
- A 是表面，B 是答案。

Example style:

- 流走的是时间，长出来的是自己。
- 不是更多选择，而是更准判断。
- 少一点讨好，多一点回来。
- 把焦虑放下，把身体接住。

Why it works:

- Symmetry increases fluency, memory, and visual headline force.

Avoid:

- Do not force couplets or rhyme.
- Do not sacrifice accuracy for neatness.
- Do not make the line feel like a cheap festival couplet.

### Pattern: 情绪桥接

Use when:

- The brand needs resonance, warmth, or social sharing.
- A manifesto, social post, festival copy, or emotional poster must connect personal feeling to brand value.

Core mechanism:

- Start from a true subtle emotion, then bridge it to product meaning, brand behavior, or a relationship with the user.

Structure:

- 不是 A 难，而是 B 更难。
- 也许你不是 A，你只是 B。
- 每个人都在说 A，很少有人看见 B。
- 当你 [具体场景]，才发现 [真实情绪]。
- 我们不是想让你 A，而是想陪你 B。
- [产品/品牌] 不只是 A，也是 B。
- 如果 [情绪状态] 有一个出口，它应该是 [品牌动作/场景]。

Example style:

- 不是不想努力，是太久没有被理解。
- 也许你不是不够坚强，你只是太久没有被允许先照顾自己。
- 每个人都在问结果，很少有人看见你撑过来的过程。
- 一瓶香气不只是好闻，也是夜里终于能慢下来的一口气。

Why it works:

- People share copy that says a feeling for them without over-explaining it.

Avoid:

- Do not use emotion as traffic bait.
- Do not force sadness.
- Do not speak down to the user.
- Do not let emotion float away from product, scene, or brand value.

### Pattern: 虚实转译

Use when:

- The product benefit is sensory, atmospheric, premium, or hard to explain directly.
- The category involves fragrance, beauty, alcohol, auto, technology, real estate, lifestyle, or brand film language.

Core mechanism:

- Use a concrete anchor to hold credibility, then add imagination, metaphor, sensory space, or emotional image to expand meaning.

Structure:

- 用 [虚的意象] 表现 [实的体验]。
- 在 [真实场景] 里，长出 [想象空间]。
- 看起来是 [虚构故事]，落脚是 [真实情感/产品利益]。
- 不是直接说 [功能]，而是让用户感到 [意象化体验]。
- 用 [具体事实] 托住 [抽象价值]。
- 把 [难以描述的感官] 写成 [可想象的画面]。

Example style:

- 不是一阵香气，是夜晚边缘的一点黑色。
- 不是一台音箱，是把房间推向另一个维度的开关。
- 用一阵风，说明一个品牌在不确定里做确定的事。
- 把回甘写成人生的不甘之后，仍然愿意留下的甜。

Why it works:

- The real anchor builds trust while the imagined layer creates desire and memorability.

Avoid:

- Do not write empty premium imagery.
- Do not create a fictional image unrelated to the product.
- Do not let users remember the image but forget what is being sold.

### Pattern: 互文回声

Use when:

- A short line must carry product, emotion, and brand meaning at the same time.
- The copy needs two clauses that complete each other.

Core mechanism:

- Let the first clause open cognition, image, or emotion; let the second clause complete the brand, product, action, or value.

Structure:

- A 的是 X，B 的是 Y。
- 把 A 做到，也把 B 留下。
- 有事 A，没事 B。
- 多一些 A，少一些 B。
- 不是只为 A，也是为了 B。
- 每一个 A，都是一份 B。
- 你的 A，世界的 B。
- 从 A 出发，向 B 回来。
- A 是动作，B 是意义。

Example style:

- 涂抹的是精油，接住的是自己。
- 看见的是身体，回来的是掌控感。
- 少一点被定义，多一点重新定义。
- 不是奖励母亲，也是归还她自己。
- 每一次安静修复，都是一次向内重启。

Why it works:

- It creates a meaning loop. The reader completes the thought between the two clauses.

Avoid:

- Do not repeat the same meaning twice.
- Do not make the brand or product disappear behind wordplay.
- Do not write decorative symmetry without strategic purpose.

### Pattern: 汉语句法变形器

Use when:

- A draft is correct but flat.
- Strategy has been defined but the sentence still sounds like a summary.

Core mechanism:

- Transform a plain statement through Chinese syntax: contrast, hypothesis, choice, omission, reversal, repetition, tone, or semantic displacement.

Structure:

- 不是 A，而是 B。
- 如果 A，那么 B。
- 与其 A，不如 B。
- A 可以 B，也可以 C。
- 少一点 A，多一点 B。
- 看起来是 A，其实是 B。
- 把 A 写成 B。
- 用 A 解释 B。
- A 是表面，B 是答案。
- 没有 A，只有 B。

Example style:

- 不是回到从前，是重新长出自己。
- 身体慢下来，自己才回来。
- 别急着成为母亲，先重新成为你。
- 每一次修复，都是一次把生活握回手里。

Why it works:

- It makes the sentence feel like advertising instead of strategy notes.

Avoid:

- Do not use syntax tricks without a sharper meaning.
- Do not let puns, homophones, or reversals become the whole idea.
- Do not break logic for novelty.

## Sentence Trigger Library

Use this library for fast rough generation after the strategy, audience, product benefit, and psychological motive are clear. Sentence triggers are starters, not answers. After using them, judge the output with `copy-methodologies.md`.

### Difference / Comparison

- 比 A 更 B
- 好过 / 胜过 A
- A 才是 B / 只有 A 才能 B
- 除了 A，还有 B
- 没有 A，就没有 B
- 没有 A，只有 B

Example style:

- 比恢复更重要的，是重新拥有自己。
- 好过急着证明自己，是慢慢把自己养回来。
- 能让你重新感到自己，才是真正的恢复。

### Identity / Attitude

- 值得 A
- 只 A / 只为 B
- 就 / 就要 A
- 永不 / 绝不 / 永远不 A
- 从不 A
- 不凡 / 非凡 / 不平凡

Example style:

- 你值得一次不被打扰的修复。
- 只为那些不想被角色吞没的人。
- 永不贩卖焦虑，只提供重塑的力量。

### Fear / Relief

- 只怕 / 最怕 / 不怕
- 不必 A
- 没人 / 没有人 A
- 哪有 A
- 总会 / 终将过去

Example style:

- 不怕身体变慢，只怕再也听不见自己。
- 不必马上成为好妈妈，先慢慢成为自己。
- 没有人该替你定义恢复的样子。

### Time / Change

- 再一次 / 再出发 / 下一次
- 不再 / 再也不 A
- 从前 A，如今 B
- 今天 A，明天 B
- 第一次 A

Example style:

- 再一次，把生活握回手里。
- 从前被期待成为母亲，如今先学会成为自己。
- 第一次，不为任何角色，只为自己恢复。

### Discovery / Story / Belonging

- 发现 A
- 故事 A
- 故乡 A
- 回家 A
- 梦想 A
- 那些 A
- 你 A，你 B

Example style:

- 发现身体慢下来以后，自己反而更清楚了。
- 每一道身体的变化，都有一个没说出口的故事。
- 你照顾新的生命，你也该被温柔照顾。

### Question / Reframing

- 反问句
- 不是 A，是 B
- 越 A，越 B

Example style:

- 谁说恢复，一定是回到从前？
- 不是产后恢复，是一次自我重塑。
- 越温和，越有重新开始的力量。

Avoid:

- Do not use more than 3-5 trigger types in one generation round.
- Do not choose triggers before choosing the communication task.
- Do not let formulas replace product truth, user psychology, or channel fit.
- Do not overuse words such as `值得`, `不凡`, or `第一次` when they create a cheap inspirational tone.

## Product / Conversion Patterns

### Pattern: 功能到体感

Use when:

- The product has ingredients, technology, materials, or mechanisms that users do not naturally care about.

Core mechanism:

- Translate cold features into bodily feeling, psychological benefit, or action reason.

Structure:

- 不是 [技术参数]，而是 [用户感受到的变化]。
- 因为 [功能机制]，所以你会 [体感/心理利益]。
- 从 [问题状态] 到 [更好的状态]。

Example style:

- 不是更复杂的配方，而是让身体慢慢松下来。
- 不是一双更轻的鞋，而是跑到最后一公里时，脚还愿意继续。

Why it works:

- People do not buy parameters. They buy the change those parameters create.

Avoid:

- Do not abandon the fact. The feeling must connect back to the real feature.

### Pattern: 风险解除

Use when:

- The user is interested but hesitant.
- The category has trust barriers, safety concerns, price doubts, or usage anxiety.

Core mechanism:

- Name the worry before the user does, then answer it.

Structure:

- 你担心的不是 A，而是 B。
- 所以我们先解决 [风险]。
- 在你决定之前，先看这三个事实。

Example style:

- 你担心的不是精油有没有香味，而是它会不会给产后的身体增加负担。

Why it works:

- Trust grows when the brand can articulate the user's hesitation.

Avoid:

- Do not invent fear or exaggerate risk to sell relief.

### Pattern: 具体承诺

Use when:

- The product benefit is too abstract.
- The copy needs stronger sales force.

Core mechanism:

- Replace general benefit with a specific and believable promise.

Structure:

- 帮你 [具体结果]。
- 在 [场景/时间]，让你 [可感知变化]。
- 少一点 A，多一点 B。

Example style:

- 让夜里的护理，不再成为另一件需要咬牙完成的事。
- 少一点紧绷，多一点能慢慢回来的松弛。

Why it works:

- Specific promises are easier to believe and act on.

Avoid:

- Do not promise results the product cannot prove.

### Pattern: 前后状态

Use when:

- The copy needs to show transformation.
- The user needs to imagine the difference.

Core mechanism:

- Show the old state and the improved state.

Structure:

- 从 [旧状态] 到 [新状态]。
- 以前是 A，现在可以 B。
- 不是马上变成 A，而是慢慢回到 B。

Example style:

- 从被身体拖着走，到重新听见身体的节奏。
- 不是立刻满血复活，而是慢慢不再和自己对抗。

Why it works:

- Transformation makes value visible.

Avoid:

- Do not overpromise dramatic change when the product only supports gradual improvement.

### Pattern: 证据链递进

Use when:

- The audience needs proof before belief.
- The category is high price, high concern, or function-sensitive.

Core mechanism:

- Build credibility step by step: fact -> proof -> scene -> action.

Structure:

- 事实：[what is true]
- 证明：[why it is credible]
- 场景：[where the user feels it]
- 行动：[what to do next]

Example style:

- 事实：山茶花油脂结构温和亲肤。证明：适合做长时间贴肤护理。场景：夜间身体护理。行动：先从一处最紧绷的地方开始。

Why it works:

- It helps users move from doubt to consideration without relying on hype.

Avoid:

- Do not fake evidence or use vague proof words like trusted, professional, or scientific without support.
