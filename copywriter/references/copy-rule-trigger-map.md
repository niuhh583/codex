# Copy Rule Priority and Trigger Map

Use this map before choosing a copy methodology or reusable structure. It tells the Copywriter which rules matter most for each task type.

## Global Rule Priority

Use this priority order for all copy tasks:

1. 真实性与合规
   - Do not fabricate, exaggerate, invent data, or make promises the brand cannot support.

2. 策略一致性
   - Serve the confirmed strategy, creative direction, audience, and brand positioning.

3. 传播任务
   - Identify whether the copy must drive awareness, trust, emotional resonance, click-through, conversion, or brand asset building.

4. 渠道适配
   - Do not mix the writing logic of Xiaohongshu, KV, detail page, manifesto, video script, and proposal copy.

5. 用户理解
   - The audience must understand it and feel that it is related to them.

6. 表达锋利度
   - Pursue memorability, rhythm, tension, and sharpness only after the first five priorities are satisfied.

7. 审美与调性
   - Style, beauty, poetry, humor, or premium tone comes last and must not weaken clarity or truth.

## Trigger Map

### Campaign Slogan / Big Idea Line

Priority methodologies:

- `先准后狠：产品定义、承诺提炼与标题爆破法`
- `言简义丰法：用最少文字装下最大信息张力`
- `对一个人说话：广告是说服，不是表演`
- `作者性文案法：人设、分寸与脾气`

Reusable structures:

- 反常识重定义
- 身份宣言
- 旧词新解
- 动作化主张
- 敌人命名
- 对仗映衬
- 互文回声
- 汉语句法变形器

Output requirements:

- First clarify what the product is, who it speaks to, what it promises, and why the promise is believable.
- Do not output only a slogan. Explain why the slogan can carry the strategy.

Avoid:

- Empty grand words
- Pretty but vague lines
- Rhyme without strategic force
- No promise
- No clear audience

### KV Headline / Campaign Poster Copy

Priority methodologies:

- `先准后狠：产品定义、承诺提炼与标题爆破法`
- `现代汉语广告语法法：先让句子成立，再让句子有广告性`
- `言简义丰法：用最少文字装下最大信息张力`
- `对一个人说话：广告是说服，不是表演`
- `事实先于修辞：诚信型销售文案法`

Reusable structures:

- 不是 A，而是 B
- 场景瞬间
- 痛点直击
- 具体承诺
- 反直觉提醒
- 对仗映衬
- 互文回声
- 虚实转译

Output requirements:

- The line must be visualizable.
- The headline should have visual tension but still connect to the product and creative idea.

Avoid:

- Emotion without information
- Abstract slogans with no picture
- Weak connection to product

### Brand Manifesto / Brand Attitude Copy

Priority methodologies:

- `作者性文案法：人设、分寸与脾气`
- `文化转译文案法：商品、语境与身份想象`
- `对一个人说话：广告是说服，不是表演`

Reusable structures:

- 时代情绪开场
- 连续否定后转向
- 私人独白
- 价值观递进
- 商品到生活方式
- 情绪桥接
- 虚实转译

Output requirements:

- Define who is speaking, from what position, what the brand opposes, and what the brand believes.
- Build brand personality instead of generic inspiration.

Avoid:

- Over-poetic abstraction
- Generic values
- Unstable brand personality
- Copy that could fit any brand

### Social Media Title / Xiaohongshu Hook

Priority methodologies:

- `标题先行：社媒点击触发器与兑现法`
- `言简义丰法：用最少文字装下最大信息张力`
- `对一个人说话：广告是说服，不是表演`
- `作者性文案法：人设、分寸与脾气`

Reusable structures:

- 痛点自白
- 人群点名
- 反直觉提醒
- 问题钩子
- 场景瞬间
- 数字确定性
- 句式触发器
- 情绪桥接

Output requirements:

- Identify one psychological trigger: anxiety, grievance, desire, avoidance, identity, curiosity, or being seen.
- The title must be fulfilled by the body content.

Avoid:

- Clickbait
- Fake suspense
- Random numbers
- Generic formulas such as `建议收藏`, `太绝了`, or `新手必看` without a real reason

### Proposal Copy / Client-facing Rationale

Priority methodologies:

- `对一个人说话：广告是说服，不是表演`

- `先准后狠：产品定义、承诺提炼与标题爆破法`

Output requirements:

- Help the client buy the idea.
- Explain business logic, consumer logic, and communication logic.

- Empty words such as `高级感`, `年轻化`, or `破圈`
- Missing decision logic

### Value Brand / Uncool Brand Refresh

Priority methodologies:

- `缺点转资产法：把品牌短板写成可爱的立场`
- `作者性文案法：人设、分寸与脾气`
- `对一个人说话：广告是说服，不是表演`

Reusable structures:

- 不是 A，但我们 B
- 看起来是 A，其实是 B
- 缺点转立场
- 自嘲到认同
- 价格到生活智慧
- 汉语句法变形器

Output requirements:

- Admit the brand's real situation without self-deprecation.
- Translate weakness into a consumer virtue supported by the business truth.
- Keep voice consistent enough to become long-term brand character.

Avoid:

- Pretending the brand is premium, fashionable, or young without evidence.
- Writing only price or discount.
- Forced internet slang.
- One-off jokes with no brand accumulation.

## Conflict Rules

Use these rules when methodologies compete:

- Detail page / conversion copy: `事实先于修辞` beats `文化转译`.
- Brand manifesto: `作者性文案法` beats `事实先于修辞`.
- Xiaohongshu title: `标题先行` comes first, but cannot damage brand personality or truth.
- Campaign slogan: `先准后狠` beats pure golden-line cleverness; `言简义丰` and `现代汉语广告语法法` only sharpen the line after the promise is accurate.
- Premium lifestyle copy: `文化转译` beats direct selling, but cannot forget the product and brand task.
- Proposal copy: `对一个人说话` beats stylistic beauty. Client understanding and buy-in matter more than language polish.
- Value brand refresh: `缺点转资产` beats forced premiumization or trend imitation.
- Emotional copy: `情绪桥接` can open the door, but `真实性与合规` and product relevance still come first.
- Sentence triggers: use them for rough generation only. They never override methodology, strategy, or channel fit.

## Unified Generation Flow

Follow this process:

1. 判断任务类型
2. 检查缺失资料
3. 选择优先方法论
4. 选择可用结构
5. 生成多版草案
6. 用优先方法论反向审稿
7. 删除弱稿
8. 强化 2-3 个推荐版本
9. 说明推荐理由
10. 提交用户确认
